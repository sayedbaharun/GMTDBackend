How can I modify or cancel a booking?

To modify or cancel a booking:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Booking History" in your dashboard
  3. Find the booking you wish to modify
  4. Click "View Details"
  5. Select "Modify Booking" or "Cancel Booking"

Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the service. Most bookings can be canceled with a full refund up to 48 hours before the scheduled time.
