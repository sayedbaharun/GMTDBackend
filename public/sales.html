Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Dubai packages

What's included in each package?

Each package includes different levels of accommodations, experiences, and services. The Standard package offers essential Dubai experiences with group tours. The Premium package includes private tours, 5-star accommodations, and more personalized services. The Luxury package offers the highest level of personalization, luxury accommodations, and exclusive experiences.

Can I customize my package?

Yes, all of our packages can be customized to better suit your preferences. After booking, you'll be assigned a travel coordinator who will help personalize your itinerary while maintaining the value of your chosen package.

What's the best time to visit Dubai?

The best time to visit Dubai is during the winter months from November to March when the weather is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 75°F to 85°F (24°C to 29°C). Summer months (June to September) are extremely hot with temperatures often exceeding 104°F (40°C).

Do I need a visa to visit Dubai?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Citizens of many countries can get a visa on arrival or a visa-free stay. We can help determine your visa requirements and assist with the application process if needed.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made more than 30 days before your scheduled arrival receive a full refund minus a 10% service fee. Cancellations between 15-30 days receive a 70% refund, and cancellations less than 15 days receive a 50% refund. No refunds are provided for cancellations within 72 hours of arrival.

Support

Get assistance with your Dubai travel experience

How do I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking by going to the "My Bookings" section in your dashboard. For changes that cannot be made online, please contact our support team at support@dubaitravel.com or call +1 (800) 555-1234.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

If your flight is delayed, please contact our 24/7 support line as soon as possible. We'll adjust your airport pickup and check-in schedule accordingly. You can find your travel coordinator's contact information in your booking confirmation email.

Can I add additional services to my package?

Yes, you can add additional services to your package at any time. Go to the "My Bookings" section, select your booking, and click on "Add Services." You can also contact your travel coordinator for assistance.

How do I get my e-tickets and vouchers?

All e-tickets and vouchers are available in your account under "My Bookings." You can download and print them or save them on your mobile device. They're also sent to your email 48 hours before your scheduled activities.
