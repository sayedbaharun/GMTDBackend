Support

Get assistance with your Dubai travel experience

How do I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking by going to the "My Bookings" section in your dashboard. For changes that cannot be made online, please contact our support team at support@dubaitravel.com or call +1 (800) 555-1234.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

If your flight is delayed, please contact our 24/7 support line as soon as possible. We'll adjust your airport pickup and check-in schedule accordingly. You can find your travel coordinator's contact information in your booking confirmation email.

Can I add additional services to my package?

Yes, you can add additional services to your package at any time. Go to the "My Bookings" section, select your booking, and click on "Add Services." You can also contact your travel coordinator for assistance.

How do I get my e-tickets and vouchers?

All e-tickets and vouchers are available in your account under "My Bookings." You can download and print them or save them on your mobile device. They're also sent to your email 48 hours before your scheduled activities.
